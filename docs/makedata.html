Building Digital Thread Models

The STEPNode package has several examples showing how to build digital thread models from a variety of source material. STEPNode is the Node plugin used by NC.js to access the digital thread.

The examples demonstrate how to import CAM data from Siemens NX and build complete digital thread models from a variety of sources.

You can adapt these Node programs however you like to tailor the digital thread models to your specific preferences.
